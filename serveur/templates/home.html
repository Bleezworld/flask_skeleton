Why backup your Google Drive data?

Google Drive is excellent and you should use it everywhere: it removes the need for Microsoft Office and is perfect for having all your files in sync accessible from anywhere.

However, and you know it because it already happened to you or someone close to you, files can disappear.

Someone could have shared something with you and unshared it, you could have trashed it, or Google can have an interruption of service and you need that file right now!
